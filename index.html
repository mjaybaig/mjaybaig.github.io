Movie Stars

This Web app demonstrates a prototype visualization of the IMDB Movies dataset, and attempts to drill down from genres to eras, and from eras to groups of movies within each year. It ultimately aims to show movies from each era and genre that are in “a league of their own” and how they compare to other top movies within that subset.
